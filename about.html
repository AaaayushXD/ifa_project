About Us

Welcome to Food Delight, a culinary journey through the heart of authentic Nepali and Newari flavors, nestled in the vibrant city of Kathmandu since 2004 AD. At Food Delight, we take pride in serving a delectable array of traditional dishes that capture the essence of Nepal's rich gastronomic heritage. Our menu is a testament to the diverse and delicious culinary traditions that have been passed down through generations, offering an unforgettable dining experience for locals and visitors alike. From savoring the aromatic spices of Newari delicacies to indulging in timeless Nepali favorites, every bite at Food Delight is a celebration of taste, culture, and tradition. We carefully select the finest ingredients to craft each dish, ensuring an authentic and memorable culinary adventure.

Join us at Food Delight and embark on a gastronomic voyage that pays homage to Nepal's culinary roots, all within the charming ambiance of Kathmandu. Our seasoned chefs and warm staff eagerly await the opportunity to serve you a taste of our nation's finest offerings. Come, savor, and relish the magic of Nepal on a plate, only at Food Delight.
